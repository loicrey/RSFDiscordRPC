RSFDiscordRPC

Represent our beloved rally simulation on Discord.

RSFDiscordRPC is a simple RallySimFans.hu plugin that brings detailed Discord Rich Presence to Richard Burns Rally and RallySimFans plugin.

Download zip

What it does

A few key points are all you need to know.

State detection

The plugin detects whether you are in the menus, watching a replay, or driving a stage.

State details and images

When on stage, the Rich Presence shows the stage and car details together with matching images.

Simple installation

Download the zip and extract its contents into the game's Plugins folder.

Contributing

RSFDiscordRPC is open source. If you want to help improve it, contributions are welcome.

Features and fixes

Suggestions, bug fixes, cleanup, and new features are welcome through issues and pull requests.

Track and car images

You can also contribute missing or improved stage and car images to help complete the Rich Presence image set.
